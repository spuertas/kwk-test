Get ready to obtain your
CODING SUPERPOWERS!

Kode with Klossy is a 2-week program that teaches girls ages 13-18 programming skills.

What languages will I learn?

For the first week, you'll learn Ruby on Rails. During week 2, you'll learn the basics of HTML, CSS, and Sinatra! At the end of the program you will be able to create your very own web app.

What does a typical day look like?

You will arrive at camp by 9AM, and start with a warm-up activity. Throughout the day you'll learn new concepts and do fun labs! There are breaks every so often to play games like Wa, Yeehaw, Ninja, and more! Lunch is around 12 (more info about lunch below).

What do I need to bring? Do I need to have any prior experience?

You can bring your own laptop or borrow one from us. A notebook is also handy for notes, depending on how you learn! Kode with Klossy accepts all levels of experience--- none, or lots!

Do I need to bring my own lunch?

Nope! Healthy, brain-power-providing food is catered every day. If you have any food restrictions, we can accomodate.
